About Us

Welcome to our hostel, a comfortable and affordable home away from home! We take pride in providing top-notch facilities to make your stay enjoyable and hassle-free.

What We Offer

Our hostel comprises:

  • Spacious and well-ventilated rooms with attached bathrooms
  • High-speed Wi-Fi connectivity
  • Common lounge areas for relaxation
  • 24/7 security and CCTV surveillance
  • Ample parking space

Fees

Our fee structure is competitive and budget-friendly:

  • Single occupancy: ₹90,000 per year
  • Double occupancy: ₹60,000 per year
  • Shared Dormitory: ₹40,000 per year
*All fees include utilities and Wi-Fi charges.

Food Facilities

We provide wholesome and delicious meals in our in-house dining area:

  • Breakfast, lunch, and dinner included in the annual fee
  • Vegetarian and non-vegetarian options available
  • Special diet requests accommodated upon prior notice
  • Snacks and beverages available for purchase
